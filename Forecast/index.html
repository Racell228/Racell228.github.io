Why it augments business teams

A fully managed Service

Forecasting as a service means full automation for data pipeline set-up, ML models’ training and prediction without any manual interventions.

Exception based forecasting

An ML-enabled forecast is of such high-quality, that business experts review and correct only exceptions. That eventually leads to significantly improving KPIs: higher forecast accuracy, less forecast bias, less time on the process

People empowerment

The functionality of the platform allows business users to manage input data themselves in a plug-and-play manner. If they wish to, they can upload various data sets manually in seconds and set schedules for data refresh.
