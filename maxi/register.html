How to register?

You can register and get access to the SMS simulator web panel in a few simple steps

1

Follow the link to fill out the registration form

2

Enter the login with which you will log in to the simulator panel

3

Also create a password. It must be 8 characters long

4

Enter your name. This way we will know how to contact you in further communication

5

Your phone number in international format

6

Your email address through which we can contact you

7

Confirm your agreement with the terms of use of our smpp simulator

8

Complete the registration by clicking on the blue button

9

Now, to enter your account, enter the username and password that you specified during registration

10

Click on the Sign in button
