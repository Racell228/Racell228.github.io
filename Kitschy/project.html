overview

kitschy is a woman-led agency that was created to spread the look good / feel good movement. in a world bombarded with sh*t products that harm us and our environment, we choose to only represent ethical brands with beautiful stories to tell. we believe that good products can uplift people’s spirits and contribute to a positive lifestyle. as such, we create unique branding for products that is not only aesthetically pleasing, but that actually converts into sales. we also come from the performance-based marketing industry, which means our single most important metric is acquisition.
