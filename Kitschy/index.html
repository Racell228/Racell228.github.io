hi! meet kitschy!

we are a branding & marketing agency that focuses on conscious beauty and wellness brands. we are visual storytellers who create cool content and campaigns with the purpose of building online communities that we monetize into sales.

who are we?

kitschy is a woman-led agency that was created to spread the look good/feel good movement. in a world bomarded with trash products and soul-less brands that harm us and our environment, we choose to only represent ethnical brands with beautiful stories to tell. we believe that good products can uplift people’s spirits, boost their self-esteem, and consequently contribute to a positive lifestyle. we don’t want to be just another agency, we have a message to spread. we choose to empower people to be & feel their ultimate best. and let’s be real, when you look good, you feel damn good!

how do we do that?

coming from a performance-based background, we provide a truly unique approach to online marketing that involves a cross-platform strategy on difference social media channels. our key metric is acquisition, which means our emphasis is to bring down your cost-per-sale and maximize the life-time value of your customers. how do we do that? we expose people to your brand through engaging and interactive ads, which we then use to build an online audience. we then aim to monetize this audience by turning a big chunk of these people into buying customers! through good branding and engagement, we then focus our efforts to improve the return rate of these customers, and ultimately increase brand loyalty

what exactly do we handle?

we turn the vision you have for your brand into a reality by:

  • branding the image, voice, and creative direction of your brand

  • building a highly-converting e-commerce store that is also aesthetically beautiful

  • identifying your target market on each one of the social media platforms: facebook, instagram, email, native, and emailing

  • pumping out creative assets that we use to build out your social media strategy as well marketing campaigns

  • launching marketing campagins on all these platforms and gathering data

  • analyzing the data to understand who your ideal target is and how we can bring down your cost per acquistion

  • optimizing our marketing campaigns to reach the desired ROI

  • scaling results and maximizing sales!

founder

tracy is a digital media strategist with extensive experience in e-commerce and subscription-based products in the beauty and cosmetics world. Her career in online marketing began in affiliate marketing where she learnt the importance of data and how important it is to understand numbers to save money and maximize results. Her instinct to discover and strategize marketing breakthroughs and trends has led her to co-found Media Burn, a performance-based agency specializing in eCommerce and influencer match-ups where she served as a Chief-Operating Officer.

Her passion to create was not satisfied fully, so she decided to launch Kitschy where she can oversee the creative direction of brands she loves and continue to do what she’s good at doing, marketing! Today, she’s living the life she exactly wants between Los Angeles and Montreal, and manages her dream team with a strong ethos for women seizing professional opportunities with gusto, and hopefully empowering them to turn their dreams into reality through the power of manifestation and a ton of hard work!
