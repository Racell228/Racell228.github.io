Solutions for SMS marketing

Our main products

Sempico Solutions is one of the best sms services, which provides high quality routes all over the world. Our managers will help you to choose the best way of sending notifications, transactional or promotional sms, even if you know nothing about how it works. The main Sempico products are:

High quality SMS routes

Specially for OTP, OTT and marketing SMS

HLR and MNP Lookup service

These services exist for checking different features, like: which country and which network cell number belongs to, is it valid or not, also if the number was ported to another network etc.

Our mass texting service can provide high quality routes with attractive rates, because we work with operators directly and have a lot of benefits because of this cooperation. Also, we work with local HUBs in a lot of countries, and it helps to be competitive on the market. SMS services for business is what you need, because it’s one of the best opportunities to get good results and to increase the number of customers.

Service features

Let’s talk about future opportunities, which you will get when become our client:

User Friendly cabinet

Our service is powered by Gatum.io system, which was developed for customer’s comfort. You will have your own web cabinet with a lot of features after registration:

  • You can clean your database by HLR serviceand add not valid numbers to black list.
  • Also, you can program your sending anytime you want, so you don’t need to be near the PC all the time.
  • If you have your own website, you can connect to our service by GET API or REST API to automate your sms business.
  • Our own marketing tool will help you to be fully armed, because you can shorten your links and measure CR in your own cabinet after sending your bulk, no need to use third party apps.
  • Quality routes give you the ability to choose Sender names to reach your users.
  • You can stop or pause your campaign, if you did something wrong or want to reschedule your bulk.
