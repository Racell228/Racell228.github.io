AURA at

THE NOTRE DAME BASILICA

A LIGHT AND SOUND EXPERIENCE IN THE HEART OF THE BASILICA

MONTREAL, QUEBEC

The Fabrique de la paroisse Notre-Dame de Montréal asked Moment Factory to create a permanent experience with sound, light and video that would invite visitors to discover the Notre-Dame Basilica in old Montreal in a whole new way. Light, original orchestral scores, and grandiose architecture unite to create a multimedia show whose captivating energy takes visitors from floor to ceiling, and beyond.

- Moment Factory

Troublemakers had the mandate to compose the orchestral score, produce and run the recording sessions, compliment the video projections with sound effects and carry out the on site integration.
