ABOUT

Troublemakers is an independent and creative hub for all things audio: music and sound. We feed on artistic diversity and innovation and team up with creators sharing the desire to express an idea, an emotion that defies time or trends. Our goal is simple: add music and sound to fulfill our collaborators quest to craft the perfect experience.”
