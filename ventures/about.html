EXPERIENCE and Investments

Investment strategy

Fundamental Global seeks to invest in any type of asset whose book value exceeds its present market value. It gives added advantage by building on present-day shareholder equity through the active involvement of its principals and their decades of investing, dealmaking and operational experience.

Today, Fundamental Global’s largest holdings include GreenFirst Forest Products, Ballantyne Strong, BK Technologies and FG Financial Group. Additionally, Fundamental Global retains stakes in Firefly with several meaningful new investments underway.

Put simply, low-risk, high-reward investments – regardless of size, sector, or form of ownership-- are Fundamental Global’s specialty.
