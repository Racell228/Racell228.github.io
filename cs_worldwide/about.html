Meet us

Who we are?

Cloud Services is a consultancy company that specializes in data and analytics. We are headquartered in Ljubljana, Slovenia, and also have additional offices in Ukraine and Estonia. For over 15 years, we have been empowering businesses across Eastern and Southern Europe with advanced data solutions and tailored analytics strategies. As a trusted Microsoft partner, we help customers adopt Microsoft data and analytics products, modernize data warehouses, and navigate the entire cloud adoption cycle.
