The SCRUGE Platform is a service for creating loyalty programs that take advantage of augmented reality technology.


With the SCRUGE AR app, young audiences complete AR quests and collect coupons in augmented reality. If you want to draw them into your business, sign up to the SCRUGE platform. There, you can create coupons, follow your statistics, and get all the necessary information on how to increase the loyalty of your young audience.


It’s simple, too. You can create your own loyalty program within 15 minutes. An easy-to-use design tool helps you create discount coupons and welcome bonuses and place them in your business space in augmented reality.


At the moment, the beta test of the project is underway, so the conditions for companies are very profitable. If you become a partner at the testing stage, you’ll receive a unique offer during the global release.
