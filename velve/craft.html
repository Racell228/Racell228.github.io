Innovation isn’t just about what’s new, it’s about what’s next.

Rooted in the wisdom of African botanicals and elevated by cutting-edge science, our proprietary formulas set a new global standard in high-performance skincare. AquaNile Brew™: The Future of Hydration

A skincare revolution, years in the making. Inspired by the life-giving waters of the Nile, our proprietary AquaNile Brew™ redefines hydration at its core. Powered by Moisture-Intelligent™ actives, it merges bio-fermented African botanicals with next-generation delivery systems to drench skin in deep, lasting moisture.

The science

Engineered with rich bioactive African botanical compounds, AquaNile Brew™ enhances the skin’s natural hydration mechanisms. Our Moisture-Intelligent™ lipid carriers mimic and strengthen the skin’s natural moisture barrier, while our Microfluidic Delivery System—a state-of-the-art penetration technology—identifies areas of depletion, replenishes moisture reservoirs, and delivers hydration deep into the skin, boosting elasticity and cellular water retention.

African Black Soap Technology™: Tradition, Transformed

Revered for centuries. Reinvented for today. Our proprietary African Black Soap Technology™ is a first-of-its-kind innovation that modernizes one of Africa’s most powerful skincare secrets. Powered by our Encapsulated Purification System, we’ve transformed African Black Soap into a high-performance cleansing system that purifies without stripping, refines without irritation, and restores balance for a clearer, smoother, more radiant complexion.

The science

Designed for superior skin compatibility, our groundbreaking formulation optimizes pH to skin-friendly levels, preventing irritation. Our Encapsulated Purification System cleanses deeply while preserving the skin’s microbiome, while our Adaptive Sebum-Regulating Complex, enriched with bioactive African botanical compounds, balances oil production, refines texture, and minimizes breakouts—without over-drying.

The future of skincare starts here

Each Velvé formulation is a testament to our belief that African botanicals, paired with cutting-edge innovation, set a new benchmark for transformative results. We are shaping the future—creating skincare that is as exquisite as it is effective.
