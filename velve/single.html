Details & Benefits

Meet The Holy Grail of Cleansers

Like you, it’s a multi-tasker; working hard, playing hard, and never compromising on excellence.

At the heart of this innovation lies our proprietary African Black Soap technology™, meticulously crafted to deliver all the purifying benefits of this revered skincare ritual, without the harshness and dryness, making it gentler on your skin. pH-balanced and deeply nourishing, this cleanser is infused with hydrating Plantain Peel and Babassu Oil effortlessly dissolves makeup, pollutants, and impurities without stripping your skin’s essential moisture.

This silky gel formula, enriched with illuminating botanicals, refines pores, soothes breakouts, and enhances skin clarity. Antioxidant-rich Roasted Cacao Pods and Green Tea work to visibly reduce redness, dark spots, and hyperpigmentation, unveiling a brighter, more even-toned complexion.

Who It’s For

pH-balanced (pH: 5.1 – 6.1) and suitable for all skin types, including sensitive skin.

Especially recommended for those concerned with:

  • Blemishes & breakouts

  • Premature signs of aging

Key ingredients

Roasted Cacao Pods – Potent antioxidant that shields skin from damage and premature aging. Rich in anti-inflammatory properties to calm redness and irritation.

Plantain Peel – Naturally enriched with Vitamin A to support skin cell re-generation, leaving your complexion soft, smooth, and refined.

Babassu Oil – A deeply nourishing emollient that replenishes moisture for lasting suppleness.

Green Tea – A powerhouse antibacterial ingredient that detoxifies pores, fades dark spots, and promotes a radiant glow.

Glycolic Acid – A gentle exfoliant that smooths skin texture, boosts collagen, and protects against sun damage.

Hyaluronic Acid – The ultimate hydration hero, drawing in moisture for plump, dewy skin.

Full Ingredients

How To Use

Designed for daily indulgence, our cleanser is gentle enough for twice-daily use (AM/PM).

  • Dispense a dime-sized amount onto damp skin.
  • Gently massage in circular motions to activate its potent, skin-nourishing ingredients.
  • Rinse thoroughly with lukewarm water and gently pat dry with a soft cloth.
  • For optimal results, follow with our Baobab & Rose Hydra-Dew Cream for lasting hydration.

Insider Tip: Need extra TLC? Our cleanser doubles as a restorative mask treatment. Apply a generous layer over damp skin and let it work its magic for 3-5 minutes before rinsing. For an enhanced sensorial experience, wipe away with a warm, damp cloth.
