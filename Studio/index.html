About Us

Brandon Studio represents a philosophy of mindful and distinctive design. Our Interior Designers take on the responsibility of creating environments that enhance the beauty and functionality of our client's interior spaces. As qualified design professionals, we offer a wide range of services such as, design consultations, space planning, contractor management, art curation and more. Brandon provides access to exclusive and rare products that are not generally available to the public. Together, we can design an environment that is as unique as you are.
