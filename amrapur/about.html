About Amrapur

Amrapur Overseas started in California in 1983 by father and son duo, Harry and Chandru “Kevin” Wadhwani. Initially, we began as a supplier of institutional andhospitality linens and later expanded into retail home fashion textiles and décor items.

About us Our history About us Our history About us Our history

Amrapur Overseas was founded in California in 1983 by the father-son team of Harry and Chandru “Kevin” Wadhwani. Initially, the company supplied institutional and hospitality linens, but later expanded into retail home fashion textiles and décor. From the beginning we have taken pride in building relationships that go beyond the product itself. Our extensive experience and diverse product range have been crucial in evolving relationships with partners, enabling them to achieve growth beyond their expectations.

Our journey started in 1960 when Harry M. Wadhwani began developing textiles in Hong Kong. From that point, we shifted our focus to sourcing, earning a reputation for excellence in fashion apparel, home textiles, and décor. Amrapur has expanded its reach beyond North America, actively selling to markets in Mexico, Latin America, the UK, Europe, Dubai, and the Middle East.

Mission

As Amrapur has grown we have acquired top talent in sales, design and beyond. Our dedicated sales staff works hand in hand with our Design team to exceed customer expectations down to the finest details. Our experienced design team in California continually researches and develops in line with the latest trends, always keeping an eye on what’s next. Our commitment to high standards and stringent quality control ensures an exceptional product is delivered on time, every time.

At Amrapur we are committed to exceeding expectations of both the client and retail customer. With our vast knowledge and meticulous research, we deliver valuable insights and meet needs effectively and efficiently.
