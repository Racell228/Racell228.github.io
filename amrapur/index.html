Who we are

Amrapur Overseas was founded in California in 1983 by the father-son team of Harry and Chandru “Kevin” Wadhwani. Initially, the company supplied institutional and hospitality linens, but later expanded into retail home fashion textiles and décor. From the beginning we have taken pride in building relationships that go beyond the product itself. Our extensive experience and diverse product range have been crucial in evolving relationships with partners, enabling them to achieve growth beyond their expectations.
